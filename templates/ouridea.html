Our Project

The Idea

Our Idea for this project is to showcase the famous Marvel superheroes we all know and love, and showcase who they are and what they do

How Does It Work?

Utilizing our API we used for this project, all you have to do to navigate the project is to simply see a superhero, click on it, and then learn some information about them and some things they did

What Happened to your old idea?

For an escape room game, it was going to take a lot more time and effort put into it than originally anticipated, so we had to cut back to a showcase as a result. So while it was a creative idea to make an adventure game for a project, it was a lot more time-consuming and advanced than we anticipated
